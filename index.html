About Me:

I was born in France , spent my childhood in Africa , went to college in Paris , then got a job at the United Nations in New York and finally made my way to Columbia journalism school.

For many years, my world was all about the news: I was a technology reporter at Reuters, then a news editor at Bloomberg in New York, breaking news on Samsung and editing stories about Vladimir Putin's billionaire friends.

But... ... ... Every year I found myself more intrigued by the "other side" of journalism: the technology that was powering the financial databases and news terminals . So I rolled up my (mental) sleeves and started down the road to coding .

Learning to code is HARD -- and such a joy at the same time! Over the past few years I've learned to work with JavaScript including React.js and Node.js ; Git and GitHub ; HTML5 and CSS ; SQL and Python . So much to learn, so little time!
